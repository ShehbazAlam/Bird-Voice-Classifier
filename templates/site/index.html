About the Project

The Bird Voice Classifier is a cutting-edge tool designed to identify bird species by their calls. Using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, our classifier can accurately recognize and categorize various bird calls, making bird watching and research easier and more enjoyable.

Technologies Used:

  • Machine Learning
  • Neural Networks
  • Audio Processing

Comprehensive Database

Includes a vast database of bird calls from around the world.

How It Works

Our bird voice classifier works in three simple steps:

  1. Record or upload a bird call.
  2. Our system analyzes the audio using machine learning algorithms.
  3. Receive the identification result with detailed information about the bird species.
